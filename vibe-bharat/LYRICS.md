तू यूज़र, लूज़र, यहीं तेरा क्रॉस।

छोड़ दे यार, ये अंग्रेज़ी बीमारी,

## 3. Mixed Translation (Hindi & English)
*A strategic translation combining original concepts with English meaning.*

**Naipaul sahi kehte the...** (Naipaul was right...)  
**"The world is what it is."**  
**Duniya reham nahi karti.** (The world has no mercy.)  
**Aur hum?** (And us?)  
**Hum abhi kuch nahi.** (We are nothing yet.)

**Andar ki aag, ab baahar la!** (Bring that inner fire out!)  
**Become the flame, do the work.**  
**Our India, our name, our Tricolor flying high.**

**Kaagazi azaadi (paper-freedom) is not enough.**  
**We will build a new foundation.**  
**Deeptech, Rockets, AI and Chips...**  
**How long will we beg for technical crumbs (takneeki bheek)?**

**Chhod Dates, Flings... (Leave the distractions...)**  
**Ye pyaar nahi, ye fokat time loss.** (This isn't love, it's a waste of time.)  
**Matrix se nikal.** (Exit the Matrix.)  
**Do service for the nation (Desh Seva) for all, all, ALL!**

**System ke bug ko bana feature tu khaas.** (Take the bugs of the system and make them your feature.)  
**Write history on your own terms (Apne dam pe likh itihaas).**  
**Before you are the Guru, you must be the 'Vishwa-Chela' (World-Disciple).**

**Tapna padega.** (You must burn.)  
**Ladna padega.** (You must fight.)  
**Wear your fire. Jai Hind. Jai Bharat.**
