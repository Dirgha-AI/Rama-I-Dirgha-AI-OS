# World ERP: The Universal Business OS

## Beyond Chat: A 360° Digital Infrastructure

Dirgha AI OS is not just an interface for AI agents; it is a **Universal Business Operating System**. While most AI companies are building narrow tools, Dirgha has already scaffolded the digital infrastructure for an entire global economy.

Our API gateway currently handles hundreds of specialized domains, allowing agents to operate with deep vertical context across every major industry.

### 🧬 The "World ERP" Thesis

In the 20th century, companies bought siloed software (SAP for ERP, Salesforce for CRM). In the 21st century, the **Dirgha World ERP** unifies these functions into a single cognitive kernel.

Because the data for Manufacturing, HR, Sales, and Research all live on the same **Agentic OS**, our agents can perform cross-domain reasoning that is impossible in legacy systems. 

**Example:** A Dirgha Manufacturing agent doesn't just track inventory; it can see a spike in sales (Commerce), cross-reference it with recruitment needs (HR), and autonomously propose a factory expansion plan (Manufacturing) while managing the financing (Capital).
