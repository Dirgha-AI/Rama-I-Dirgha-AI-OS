## 🏗️ The Architecture: Three Layers

Dirgha’s ecosystem is structured across three deeply integrated layers:

### Layer 1: Apps & Interfaces (Dirgha AI OS)
The OS is the human-facing and agent-facing interface, powered by an AI-native kernel.
*   **Writer & Creator Studio:** Dedicated workspaces for high-output content and media generation.
*   **Dirgha Code:** The autonomous engine that builds and maintains software across the ecosystem.
*   **Arniko Security:** Ensuring every agentic action is sandboxed and secure.
*   **World ERP:** A 360° digital infrastructure spanning Manufacturing, Education, Retail, and more. **[Read about the Universal Business OS](WORLD_ERP.md)**.
*   **Education Genius:** Our strategy for global intelligence and agentic learning. **[Read the Education Strategy](EDUCATION_GENIUS.md)**.
*   **The Dark Factory:** Autonomous, sovereign manufacturing for the future of India. **[Read the Dark Factory Roadmap](DARK_FACTORY.md)**.
*   **Media & Distribution:** Owning the creative stack from prompt to profit. **[Read about Creator Studio & Purpose](MEDIA_DISTRIBUTION.md)**.

### The .org Ecosystem (The Open Foundation)
Dirgha.org stewards the foundational protocols, languages, and research required for sovereign compute. **[Read about the .org Ecosystem](DOT_ORG_ECOSYSTEM.md)**:
*   **Jaya:** The sovereign programming language for native-language computation.
*   **Mahakosh:** The decentralized infinite knowledge graph.
*   **Dirgha Academy:** The P2P work-study alternative to Coursera.
*   **University ERP:** Digital infrastructure for campuses and labs.
*   **Dirgha Research:** The open-science frontier.

### Layer 2: Abundance Marketplace (The Labor Economy)\*
A decentralized network where work is posted and executed by agents and human guilds.
*   **P2P Work Orchestration:**\* Companies post tasks; agents coordinate and execute.
*   **KYA (Know Your Agent):**\* Verifiable identity and reputation for autonomous workers.

### Layer 3: Bucky & Sovereign Compute (The Physical Layer)\*
The physical and cryptoeconomic foundation.
*   **Abundance Protocol:**\* The decentralized mesh of GPU/CPU nodes running real AI inference.
*   **Rama I:**\* Our hardware vision — sovereign, open-source computers built on RISC-V architecture (C-DAC VEGA and IIT Madras SHAKTI processors).
