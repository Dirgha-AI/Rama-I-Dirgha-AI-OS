# Investor Pitch: The Agentic Infrastructure Play

## 1. The Opportunity: Software is no longer scarce
In 2026, AI generates, tests, and deploys code faster and cheaper than any human team. Software is becoming a commodity. The real value has moved to **application and orchestration**—to the infrastructure that coordinates AI agents, humans, and compute to solve real-world problems.

**Dirgha is building the rails for this new economy.** We are a vertically integrated company spanning Computers (Rama I), an Agentic OS (Dirgha AI OS), and a decentralized labor/compute marketplace (Abundance).

## 2. Our Stand: Vertical Integration vs. The Silos
While the giants (NVIDIA, OpenAI, AWS) fight to protect their proprietary silos, Dirgha integrates horizontally across the entire stack:
*   **From Atoms:** Sovereign RISC-V hardware (Rama I).
*   **To Bits:** A cognitive OS kernel and a P2P compute protocol (Abundance).

We don't just sell software; we provide the **end-to-end infrastructure** that makes companies autonomous.

## 3. The Hybrid Business Model: Multi-Stream Revenue
Dirgha operates a high-margin, scalable business model that captures value at every layer of the agentic stack.

### A. Software & Subscriptions (Dirgha LLC, Wyoming)
We charge for the tools that humans and agents use to work.
*   **SaaS Licenses:** Three-tier subscriptions — **Pro** ($20/mo, 4K credits), **Build** ($100/mo, 20K credits), **Scale** ($200/mo, 40K credits) — spanning Chat, IDE, Writer Studio, Creator Studio\*, and vertical ERP modules (Manufacturing, Education, CRM, ATS).
*   **BYOK:** Bring-your-own-key mode — users provide their own Anthropic / OpenAI / OpenRouter keys and bypass inference billing (still gated by plan tier).
*   **Managed Services:**\* White-glove deployment of Sovereign Nodes and on-prem AI infrastructure.

### B. Marketplace & Protocol Fees (Bucky Foundation)\*
We capture a percentage of all economic activity running on our rails.
*   **Transaction Commissions:**\* A programmatic split — **70% to workers/guilds, 20% to the platform/upstream, and 10% to the DAO treasury/Sovereign Fund.**
*   **Inference Rents:**\* We route compute through the Bucky mesh, capturing value through network velocity and staking.
*   **Upstream Royalties:**\* Our Code Block registry ensures that when an agent uses a piece of code, the protocol facilitates the value flow, taking a small cut for maintenance and security (Arniko).

### C. Owned Brands & Verticals
By building on our own stack, we launch high-value vertical brands in:
*   **Digital Content:**\* High-output publishing and media houses powered by Creator Studio.
*   **Sovereign Infrastructure:**\* Providing the blueprints and OS for "City-Factories" globally.
